Why us?

We've been putting our best efforts into Casa Georgiana since '02. Hundreds of people have been our guests, most of them always returning. It's about the atmosphere, surroundings, and the strong feeling of freedom our village is thriving with.

Rarau Mountains

Weird shaped rock formations with panoramic views, the Rarau Mountains are also known as the Lady's Stones as they offer one of the most spectacular romaninan mountain scene.

Traditional houses

People

The villagers keep their traditions. Although many of them have left their native places, the old ones remain and keep the stories alive.

Close to Campulung Molovenesc

The legend says there was a big dragon that the bravest man couldn't defeat. Until one day, when a young man named Hălăuceanu killed the beast. His reward was freedom and land, and then looking for a girl and a place to sit, he arrived in the land today named of Câmpulung Moldovenesc , exclaiming with sincere astonishment: "Oh, what a long field!" (In romanina, Campulung meand "Long Field")
